Diploma in Health Inspector

Offered by Jai Bharath Institute of Medical Science, Perumbavoor


Shape Your Future in Public Health

Join our 2-year Diploma in Health Inspector course, approved by the Government of Kerala, and prepare for a rewarding career in health and allied sectors. This comprehensive program is designed to develop skilled health professionals who are ready to make a difference in public health and safety.

Course Details

The Diploma in Health Inspector is a specialized, two-year program designed to train students in essential public health skills, particularly focusing on health inspection and sanitary practices. This course aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge to play a critical role in public health, from ensuring sanitary standards to preventing disease outbreaks. This course is ideal for those committed to enhancing community health and safety, with ample employment and growth opportunities both locally and globally.

Course Overview

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Plus Two (Physics, Chemistry & Biology)
  • Seats Available: 30 seats
  • Certification: Recognized by the Paramedical Council, Government of Kerala

Career Opportunities

  • • Government Health Department
  • • Hospitals and Clinics
  • • Sanitation and Waste Management Organizations
  • • Food Safety and Inspection Agencies
  • • International Health Organizations and NGOs focused on Public Health

Benefits of the Program

  • • Recognized by the Paramedical Council of Kerala, providing credential validation.
  • • High employability in both public and private health sectors.
  • • Pathways for career advancement in health administration and public health management.
  • • Opportunities for international positions with health organizations, NGOs, and more.
