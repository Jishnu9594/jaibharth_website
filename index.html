About the Diploma in Health Inspector

Join our Diploma in Health Inspector program to gain in-depth knowledge and practical experience in public health, sanitation, and disease control. This program offers a comprehensive curriculum that prepares you for a successful career as a health inspector, accredited by the Kerala Paramedical Council. contact us.

Program Curriculum

Program Structure:

  • Duration: 1-2 years
  • Core Subjects:
  • Public Health and Hygiene
  • Disease Prevention and Control
  • Environmental Sanitation
  • Health Education and Promotion
  • Practical Training: Field visits, healthcare facility exposure
  • Certification: Accredited by Kerala Paramedical Council

Application Process

To apply for the Diploma in Health Inspector:

  • Step 1: Fill out the online application form.
  • Step 2: Submit your high school diploma or equivalent.
  • Step 3: Submit a personal statement and references.
  • Step 4: Wait for your application to be reviewed.
