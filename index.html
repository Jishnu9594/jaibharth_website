Welcome to Jai Bharath Group

Jai Bharath Educational Foundation (JBEF) was established in 2001 as a charitable trust with its registered office in Thrikkakkara, Ernakulam, Kerala. The foundation’s primary objective is to stimulate intellectual awakening and develop skilled manpower that contributes effectively to national development.

JBEF has become one of the largest networks of educational institutions in Kerala, known for its academic excellence and exceptional infrastructural facilities. Despite its relatively short journey, it has established a reputation for delivering quality education and for furthering the growth of its institutions. Today, Jai Bharath is committed to serving society by establishing diverse institutes in the Ernakulam District.

We are proud of our contributions and continue to expand the educational opportunities we offer to help individuals grow, learn, and achieve their professional aspirations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible and high-quality education to individuals, empowering them with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their careers and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

Our Programs

Building a Healthier Future: Diploma in Health Inspector Program

Enroll in the Diploma in Health Inspector program under the Kerala Paramedical Council. Gain essential knowledge and practical skills to excel in public health, disease prevention, and sanitation management. Start your career in healthcare today!

Public Health and Sanitation

Gain a comprehensive understanding of public health principles, focusing on sanitation, disease prevention, and environmental health standards to ensure community health and safety.

Environmental Health

Learn about the impact of environmental factors on health, and gain skills to assess and mitigate risks through hands-on training and fieldwork.

Health Inspection Techniques

Master essential techniques for health inspection, covering food safety, water quality analysis, and hygiene standards to ensure compliance with public health regulations.
