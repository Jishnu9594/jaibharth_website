About Us

Welcome to Jai Bharath Group

Jai Bharath Educational Foundation was established in the year 2001 as a charitable trust with a registered office at Thrikkakkara, Ernakulam, Kerala, with a focused objective of bringing about intellectual awakening of people and for preparing manpower capable of contributing effectively towards national development. Jai Bharath Educational Foundation is one of the largest networks of educational institutions in Kerala. Within a short span, this group of institutions managed by the JBEF has established its reputation for academic excellence, rich infrastructural facilities, and the potential for further growth. Today we are dedicated to serving our society in diverse fields by the establishment of the following institutes Ernakulam District.

  • Jai Bharath College of Management and Engineering Technology, Perumbavoor

  • Jai Bharath School of Management Studies, Perumbavoor

  • Jai Bharath Arts & Science College Perumbavoor

  • KMM College of Management and Technology, Thrikkakara

  • Cochin College of Arts & Science, Manakkapady, Pallikara

Milestone

1
2001

Jaibharath Group of Institution Formation

Jai Bharath Group started to provide world-class career-oriented Education and Training. Engineering at Jai Bharath is about belonging to a college that strives for excellence in teaching & learning, research & scholarship, and community service.

2
2002

Arts & Science College

Jai bharath arts and science college started for imparting Quality Education imbibing scientific and artistic knowledge to the needs of the current generation. It is situated in the heart of Ernakulam district with major PG and UG courses such as B.Com., BCA, MSW, BBA, M.Com., BA English.

3
2004

Management College

Jai Bharth started its Management College (MBA) with the aim of developing business impairment among the students who would like to start their own ventures and who wish to become an entrepreneur. It is situated in Arackappady, Perumbavur.

4
2009

Engineering & Technology

Jai Bharath started its Engineering and Technology with the aim to produce quality engineers for the industrial sector with different streams of engineering like ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, computer science etc. It’s situated in Arakapadi, Perumbavoor.

5
2010

Jaibharath Institution of Community Development

The Jai Bharath Institute of community Development and Research is one of the innovative ventures undertaken by the Jai Bharath group in 2006. The research wing promotes and elevates the quality of education and research within the campus circle.

6
2011

Electro-Mech Enterprises Pvt.Ltd

EME was developed under the incentive of Jai Bharath group for helping the candidates to impart with the world leading corporates to get placed with a lump-sum salary. It’s situated near the Edapally toll.

7
2011

KMM Arts & Science College

KMM arts and science college was a step towards imparting knowledge in accordance with the needs of the current generation. Quality education is offered in both PG and UG courses like: B.Com., BBA, M.Com., MSW, and BA English etc. It’s situated in Thrikkakara.

8
2014

Cochin College

Cochin college was another milestone for the Jai Bharath group. It was an implementation of experience and success. The arts and science college offers various PG and UG courses such as B.Com., BCA, BBA. It’s situated in Pallikara.

9
2018

ADONX

In association with JAIN University, a venture of Add-on course is initiated with the regular degree programs. These job-oriented courses are designed to equip the students to face the emerging challenges in the job market and aim to mould them with employable skills.

10
2018

TIDE

An aim emphasised on the promotion of skill development and employment opportunities. We provide additional skill development programs rooted in specialised academics and industrial training to groom the skills of our students.

11
2019

Jaibharath Polytechnic

Jai Bharath made its lively presence in the field of technical education with the establishment of Jai Bharath polytechnic. We deliver dynamic teaching style incorporating the workshops, seminars and interactive sections, which makes the technical education more vibrant and standardised. Our institute is situated in Arakapadi.

12
2020

Launch of LAVENDER EDU

LAVENDER EDU provides a platform for understanding various courses offered by the institutions and corporate sectors and also bridges the gap between educational institutions and industries. A major development of Jai Bharath Group.

Vision - Mission

Leading with Purpose: Our College's Mission and Vision

Vision

Jai Bharath aims to create an institution committed to academic excellence, producing students with the ability to think critically, creatively, become technology literate and to communicate effectively. Also serve the society by providing affordable quality higher education at a times.

Mission

Empowers students with innovative learning and personal development programs, creating a higher-level teaching and research institution. Providing facilities to achieve education goals, gain professional direction, and contribute to society. Aiming to develop human and technological capabilities, culture, character, and competence to create responsible citizens.
